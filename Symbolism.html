What the Light Bulb Symbol Means

The Light Bulb commenly symbolizs an idea or brilliance, like Benjamin Franklin's brilliant idea of the lightbulb. "The light bulb is the symbol of invention and sometimes of intelligence"(Sybolism.fandom.com).The light bulb is also a symbol of light and connections.The invention of the light bulb changed the world in many ways, including facilitating the creation of large power grids, changing the social and economic structure of society and bringing other appliances into the home.
